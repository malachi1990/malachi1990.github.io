## Musing 124

Sometimes you feel weighed down and don't realize it till the weight is off. Possibly because
we just get used to it.

I don't know what it was, but today I realized how much of a weight I'd been carrying in my heart.
Don't know what it was, but thank God that burden is no longer on my soul. 

But may I not let that levity and literal light heartedness distract from honoring Him.
