## Musing 155

There were many bits of wisdom I got from my dad growing up. 

A couple of those quips are "If you're gonna be dumb, you gotta be tough" a fairly common one, and one I haven't heard as
frequently is "If you're gonna be dumb, people are gonna laugh at you". 

And as I've gotten older, I tire of online stupidity way quicker than I used to. Misrepresentations online that even just a few
years ago, I would've been gungho to step into and correct . . . I just am like "you know what, they can go be dumb elsewhere".

In biblical terms, when I was younger, I tended to the Proverb that encouraged correcting fools. As I've gotten older, the venn diagram
of fools I'm willing to correct and the people I know IRL has become almost a circle. 

Whether that is good or bad, I don't know. But I don't really regret it.
