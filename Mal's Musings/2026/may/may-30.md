## Musing 119

Enjoy the little days. I thoroughly enjoyed spending the day with my child, despite having to run
so many errands. 

We too often think that the big moments are what matter most when raising kids. And sure, they do 
matter. But often times, the collection of little moments just matter more. 

You'll never take from me the sheer joy of my child running under the hose as I water the flowers. 

Not to mention the baby nap snuggles.
